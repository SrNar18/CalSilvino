Our Restaurant

🍽️ A family restaurant
with Andorran soul

Over 12 years ago we opened Cal Silvino in La Massana, and from day one our philosophy has been the same: quality produce, honest cooking and a family welcome.

Our menu features the dishes that have made us a gastronomic landmark of the Principality: bacalao a la llauna, grilled octopus, callos, tuna tartar and grilled meat, alongside classics like homemade cannelloni or snails a la llauna.

We also host celebrations, anniversaries and corporate dinners. Get in touch and we'll take care of everything so you can just enjoy.
